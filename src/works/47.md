# description

Quantum Entanglement is a bilingual English/Spanish work of quantum erotica, a category of our invention. However, this is the domesticated version, so it is quite tame, suitable for audiences 12 and older. This piece was developed on the Scroll Cinema platform, created by Artemio Morales. In Quantum Entanglement, you will encounter many versions of the erotic adventures of María G. Each version exists simultaneously along with reviews of her story. The versions themselves become tangled, and María G moves into a multiverse of lovers in a progression of text and image that escapes the boundaries of traditional text. Her tale, which began at ELO25 during a keynote on quantum computing, is illustrated with images of the quantum bodies entangled in superpositions from our Quanta Sutra. The positions from the quantum sutra have been algorithmically calculated using the true randomness of a quantum computer.
